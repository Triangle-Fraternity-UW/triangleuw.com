Triangle Origin

In the fall of 1906, sixteen civil engineering juniors at the University of Illinois began the association that became the Triangle Fraternity. Throughout the years, Triangle Fraternity grew rapidly across the country. Nowadays, Triangle brothers study and work across various STEM fields, advance boldly the frontiers of science and technology, and extend Triangle values and mroals into the making of a better world.

UW Triangle history

Formed in 2017, our group is the only STEM exclusive social fraternity on UW campus. Our aim as a chapter is to promote academic growth and increase social interactions between the different STEM fields, thereby encouraging interdepartmental relations between both the well known and lesser known STEM fields.
