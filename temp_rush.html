WINTER RUSH 2021

Rush Triangle 2021! Winter Rush will take place starting Monday, 1/4, and will end Friday, 1/15. While recruitment will take place virtually for the health and safety of both brothers and the community, the brotherhood will function both virtually and in-person adhering to local health guidelines, as vaccinations increase and as case numbers go down. Rush Triangle Fraternity from the comfort of your home! You will be able to get to know our brothers, and can learn more about what Triangle Fraternity has to offer. Please view our calendar below for our Winter Rush event schedule!


Read Our Statement

Rushing: To join a fraternity, you must go through a “rush” period where you attend events and get to know the members of the fraternity. Our rush process is open year round. To begin rushing, come to one of the events on the calendar above and introduce yourself. Triangle requires rushees to attend at least 3 events in a quarter and expects them to have had a conversation with more than half of the current active members. Rushees are also required to attend a group interview, where active members will ask questions to get to know them better.

The Bid: If we feel like you share the values of Triangle and that you will be a good fit for the culture of our group, we'll offer you a bid a few days after your interview. If you are offered a bid you have the opportunity to join the fraternity as a "pledge" by signing your bid and returning it to us. Whether a bid is offered is determined a vote by all active members.
Note: New members are required to have a GPA of 3.0 or higher to join (excluding first quarter freshmen).

Pledging: After you accept your bid, you will begin the pledging process. Pledging lasts one quarter, during which you will get to know the active members even better and learn how the fraternity operates by attending new member education sessions.
